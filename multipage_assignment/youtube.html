Youtube Features

  1. Allows users to upload videos and they can control who can see it.
  2. Easy to use: Easy to get started, create and account, and upload a video.
  3. Integration with other Google services: Youtube is connected to Gmail.
  4. Filters: Helps you find the video you want to see with numerous filters.
  5. Subscribe: Allows you to follow specific channels and be notified when they upload a video.

Details

Advantages of using Youtube

  • Good for learning as it has many excellent educational resources.
  • Is entertaining and can be a good way to spend your free time.
  • Convenient and easy to use: simply go to the website (link is at the top of the page).
  • Connected with many other Google services, making it very convenient.

Disadvantages of using Gmail

  • You can easily get addicted and waste your time.
  • Requires internet to use.
  • Has many advertisements: You can fix this by getting an ad-blocker
